## Description

**MC Waterslides** turns water physics up to eleven: rail-style slide channels that
snap together and merge into wide lanes, RF-powered jets that push riders through
any water — even **uphill** — and enclosed tubes for loops, long drops, and vertical
shafts. Seal a glass tunnel of any shape, flip the **Flood Valve**, and ride through
the inside of your own aquarium.

**Fun-first, zero grind.** Everything crafts from copper, clay, and iron-tier
materials. One craft paves 16 blocks of slide. An iron pickaxe and a riverbank fund
a full water park.

- 🛝 **Slide Channels** — 16 dye colors + glass-clear, auto-connecting, side-by-side
  lanes merge into wide slides
- 🚀 **Jets** — ~24 blocks of current each, push riders up to 22 b/s, work uphill,
  and share RF down a touching row (wire one, power them all)
- 🕳️ **Slide Tubes** — enclosed runs, vertical drop shafts, see-through variants
- 🌊 **Flood Valve** — floods any sealed glass volume into a rideable water tunnel,
  and finds the leak when it isn't sealed
- 🏊 **Splash Pools** — soft landings that merge into any size
- 🐄 **Mobs and items ride too** — villager lazy rivers are a lifestyle
- 🔊 A water-rush soundtrack that swells with your speed, splash-downs, spray
- 🏆 Guided advancements down to a hidden one for a single 10,000-block ride
- 📖 In-game manual via Patchouli (optional), same guide on the web

**Power**: the built-in coal/water Pump House, or any RF mod's generators.
**Performance**: no chunk loaders, by design — parks span a hundred chunks and only
tick near players.

## Requirements / notes

- NeoForge, Minecraft **1.21.1** (more versions rolling out as ports finish)
- Optional: Patchouli for the in-game Park Builder's Manual
- Server-friendly: dedicated servers supported, client does the pretty parts
