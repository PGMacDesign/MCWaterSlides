MC Waterslides — Block & Recipe Spec

v1 planning doc · 2026-07-06 · PGMacDesign · recipe grids provisional; yields are the design decision

1. Material identity: copper + clay

Copper because it's abundant, early-game, and vanilla still barely uses it — this mod is finally something to do with 40 stacks of it. Clay because it's the water-adjacent resource: every riverbed and lakebed has piles, and anyone building a waterslide is by definition near water. Both dig-it-up-anywhere common, day one, no biome luck (slime was rejected for exactly that: trivial with a slime chunk, miserable without). Nothing rare appears anywhere in the mod — iron tier at most.

2. The v1 roster

#Block/ItemRoleKey properties
1Slide ChannelOpen U-channel ride surface Rail-style auto-connect (straight / 90° corner / ascending / descending); permanently waterlogged (intrinsic water); dyeable, 16 colors via tint layer (one model set); 1 block wide — riders centered through corners; side-by-side for width.
2Slide TubeEnclosed 1-block tube Same connection logic as the channel, with a lid: vertical drops, corkscrews, fully-3D twisters. Riding one = committed (no bail until an opening). Dyeable; translucent window strip so riders aren't blind.
3JetThe physics primitive 6-way facing incl. up; projects a ~24-block current through connected water/channels; fixed thrust; redstone on/off; RF powered (idle trickle + push surge, internal buffer). Energizes any water — freeform glass tubes of any cross-section are first-class slides.
4Pump HouseStandalone generator Dual mode: 20 RF/t burning furnace fuel at 5× burn (coal = 160k RF — exact energy parity with MC3DPrint's Clock Generator, double rate, half duration); ~2 RF/t passive beside flowing water. GUI: fuel slot + flame + buffer gauge.
5Water ConduitCheap RF cable Waterproof (waterloggable), ~256 RF/t, no storage. Runs along the slide's spine.
6Flood ValveFill/drain enclosed volumes RF-powered bounded flood-fill of the sealed volume it faces (~10 RF/block, cap ~32,768); toggling off drains. Reports the leak location when the volume isn't sealed. Exists because hand-filling a 4×4 tube with source blocks is misery.
7Splash PoolSlide exit Kills momentum smoothly within ~1 block; negates all landing damage on contact; rider pops out standing.

Deliberately not in v1

  • Support pylons / decorative struts — vanilla blocks do this fine.
  • Start gates — you just hop in.
  • Race timers / checkpoints — strong v2 feature, done properly with times and leaderboards.
  • Boats / slide rafts — vehicle physics vs. current is a rabbit hole; v2 candidate.

4. Blockstate & connection model

  • Channel/Tube shape property mirrors vanilla rails: north_south, east_west, four corners, four ascending variants — picked automatically from neighbors on place/update, exactly the mental model every player already has from rails.
  • Intrinsic water: waterlogged=true is permanent (placement sets it, nothing can unset it). Vanilla fluid rendering, biome water tint, fish-out-of-water edge cases all come for free.
  • Color: one blockstate/model set + a tint index driven by a color property or per-color block registration (implementation picks; 16 registrations à la concrete is the safe multi-version choice).
  • Jet is a facing block (facing = 6 directions) + powered + enabled (redstone).

6. Ship surface

  • Patchouli soft dep: the Park Builder's Manual — your first slide; jets & power; tubes & the Flood Valve; splash pools. The in-game book IS the onboarding.
  • JEI: recipes visible; no custom categories needed.
  • Creative tab: one MC Waterslides tab, roster order as above.
